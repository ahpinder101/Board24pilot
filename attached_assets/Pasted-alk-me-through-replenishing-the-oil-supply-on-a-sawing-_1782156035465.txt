Hold the base of the nozzle of the Accessory oil tank and use scissors to cut about halfway along the straight section of the nozzle.
Loosen and remove the nozzle, then remove the seal.
Tighten the nozzle back onto the oil tank.
Tilt back the machine head to access the oil tank.
Remove the rubber cap from the oil tank.
Pour 120 ml of lubricating oil into the oil tank, using the upper reference line as a guide.
Replace the rubber cap securely.
Return the machine head to its original position.
Check that the oil gauge reaches the upper reference line in the oil gauge window.
Ensure you wear protective goggles and gloves when handling lubricating oil to prevent it from coming into contact with your eyes or skin.
